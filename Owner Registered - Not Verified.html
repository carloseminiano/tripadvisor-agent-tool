🔍 How to Verify Using Photo ID

Condition: Owner_Registered_Not_Verified + Verification Method: Not_Verified

When the OSC status says Owner_Registered_Not_Verified and the Verification method is Not_Verified, then the owner has already created an affiliation but has not completed the verification.

✅ Recommendation: Recommend the Photo ID Verification. It is the most secure method and usually takes only 1 minute.

🕒 Note: If it takes longer than 1 minute, advise phone contacts to wait. No need to mention this on email.

🔄 Other Info: Delayed verifications go to the Trust & Safety pending queue. Ask them to wait up to 72 hours for a confirmation email.
